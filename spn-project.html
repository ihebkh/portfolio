Business Intelligence Project

A Business Intelligence (BI) project for SPN Company aimed at enhancing decision-making. Utilizing data warehousing, Python for ETL processes, FastAPI for creating scalable APIs, and Next.js for developing dynamic dashboards, we analyzed sales, customer interactions, and operational data. Key insights reveal areas for process optimization and strategic realignment. Recommendations are provided to help SPN leverage these insights for improved decision-making and operational efficiency, emphasizing the importance of data-driven strategies for better organizational performance.
