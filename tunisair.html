Tunisair Project

PHP web development

The project is an innovative web platform for carpooling that simplifies travel reservations by allowing users to book cars, trains, and flights within a single interface. Designed to offer a seamless user experience, the platform features an intuitive interface where users can search for available transportation options, compare prices, and make reservations effortlessly. Among its advanced features, the platform includes an intelligent chatbot that assists users by answering frequently asked questions and guiding them through the booking process, ensuring 24/7 availability of support. Additionally, the platform integrates a question forum where the community can exchange information, share travel experiences, and receive answers from other users or experts. This combination of multi-modal booking services and interactive features makes the project a comprehensive and secure solution tailored to meet the needs of all travelers.
